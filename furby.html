Welcome to the world of Furby! Inspired by the success of Tamagotchis and Giga Pets, the creators of Furby - Dave Hampton & Caleb Chung - wanted to create a new kind of virtual pet: a completely interactive animatronic pet that could talk, sing, & dance. Furby “pets” are an iconic and memorable part of many who grew up in the 2000s, myself included.

Era 1 (1998-2002) had the Furby Adult (1998) & Furby Babies (1999) lines of Furbies. They also released some Furby Friends in this Era.

Furby Adult (1998)

  • Tickle, tilt & light sensor
  • Infrared port
  • No app/voice-recognition
  • Sensitive & Friendly personalities
  • Likes to dance, hug, get tickled, & have their backs rubbed.

Furby Babies (1999)

  • Smaller size than Furby Adult (1998)
  • Learns non-furbish pre-set language quicker than Furby Adult (1998)
  • Higher-pitched voice compared to Furby Adult (1998)
  • Unable to dance
  • White eyelashes
