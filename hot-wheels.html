What Are Hotwheels?
-
□
x

History

Hot Wheels are miniature cars that are a global icon around the world today. They were created in 1968 by Mattel to take over the british counterparts. They revolutionized the toy car industry by incorporating detailed designs, lots of features, and a large variety of vehicles.

Impact

Today, Hot Wheels have expanded into many categories, from star wars to batman themed cars. There are also many Hot Wheel inspired games, movies, and even real racing events.
