About Trash Packs

Created by Moose Toys (makers of Shopkins and Grossery Gang), Trash Packs launched in 2011 as miniature collectible trash cans containing gross-out themed figures.

The line included:

  • Blind-pack trash canisters (3-5 items per pack)
  • Dumpster playsets with interactive features
  • Special editions like Toxic Revolting Trash
  • Trading cards with gameplay elements

Why Kids Loved Them

Trash Packs became a phenomenon because:

  • Exciting blind-pack experience
  • Rebellious "gross" factor parents disliked
  • 500+ unique characters to collect
  • Affordable price (~$3-5 per pack)

Note: Discontinued in 2019 to focus on Grossery Gang

Official Rarity System

Figures were categorized by:

  • Common (Green lids - 60%)
  • Uncommon (Blue lids - 25%)
  • Rare (Purple lids - 10%)
  • Ultra Rare (Metallic - 5%)

Most Valuable Today:

  1. Series 1 "King Rat" - $80-$120
  2. 2013 Comic-Con Exclusive - $200+
  3. Series 3 "Grimy Gold" - $2000+

Collector Tip:

Heavier packs often contain metal 'dumpster lid' pieces!
